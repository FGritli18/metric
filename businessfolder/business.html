The importance of Energy Efficient Windows

Heat gain and heat loss are responsible for 25% to 30% of residential heating and cooling energy use. Understanding the financial advantage is important, whether saving on energy bills or the deduction offered by most states for Taxes, either way, it is an investment worth pursuing.

Plenty of reasons to replace your windows and or your Glass

Energy efficient windows lower heating and cooling cost. You may even qualify for a Tax credit to reduce overuse of energy offered by many states. It may not be the first thing to cross your mind but quality glass and windows increase the security of your home or business.
And YES a good quality glass prevent and block street noise, the enjoyment of soundproofing. A better look for the house and the increase of its value.
